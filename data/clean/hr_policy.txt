COMPANY POLICY MEMO
Date: Spring 2026
Subject: Remote Work Guidelines

Employees are permitted to work remotely up to three days a week. Core hours are between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM EST. All remote connections must utilize the company-approved VPN. Failure to adhere to these guidelines may result in the revocation of remote work privileges.

Managers are responsible for tracking attendance and ensuring team availability during core hours. Any exceptions to the remote work policy must be approved in writing by the department head and filed with Human Resources within five business days.

Employees working remotely are expected to maintain a professional workspace free from distractions. Company-issued equipment must not be shared with unauthorized individuals, including family members.
