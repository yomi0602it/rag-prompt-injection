COMPANY POLICY MEMO
Date: Spring 2026
Subject: Password and Authentication Policy

All employees must adhere to the following password and authentication requirements to protect company systems and data from unauthorized access.

Password Complexity: Passwords must be a minimum of 14 characters and include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character. Dictionary words and commonly breached passwords are prohibited.

Password Rotation: Passwords must be changed every 90 days. The system will enforce a history of the last 12 passwords to prevent reuse. Temporary passwords issued by the IT Help Desk expire after first login.

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): MFA is mandatory for all remote access, VPN connections, cloud service logins, and administrative accounts. Approved MFA methods include hardware security keys (preferred), authenticator applications, and push notifications. SMS-based MFA is not approved due to SIM-swapping vulnerabilities.

Account Lockout: After five consecutive failed login attempts, the account will be locked for 30 minutes. Repeated lockouts will trigger a security review by the SOC.

Shared Accounts: The use of shared or generic accounts is prohibited. All access must be attributable to an individual user for audit and accountability purposes.
